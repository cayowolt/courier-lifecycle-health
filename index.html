FAQ & Methodology

How is the Health Score calculated?

Health = 40% Activation Rate + 25% RTD Conversion + 25% Courier Retention + 10% Weekly Engagement

• Activation Rate: % of applicants (last 90d) who completed their first delivery. Scored 0–55% → 0–100.
• RTD Conversion: % of applicants who reached Ready-To-Deliver status. Scored 0–45% → 0–100.
• Courier Retention: 1 minus monthly churn rate. Scored 60%–95% retention → 0–100.
• Weekly Engagement: % of 30d-active couriers who also delivered in last 7d. Scored 30%–75% → 0–100.

Data Sources

All data sourced from Wolt Snowflake production via the semantic layer:
• Onboarding funnel: PRODUCTION.INTERMEDIATE.F_COURIER_ONBOARDING — one row per courier lifetime record. Windowed on TIME_FIRST_APPLIED_UTC (last 90 days).
• Active couriers & deliveries: PRODUCTION.MART.COURIER_EARNING_METRICS joined to D_FLEETS for country mapping. PERIOD='day', is_correctly_tracked_delivery_order=TRUE.
• Churn: Couriers active in days -60 to -31 who did NOT deliver in days -30 to -1, per country.

Time window for activity metrics: rolling 30/60/90 days from today (2026-06-29).

Onboarding Substage Definitions

Applicants: TIME_FIRST_APPLIED_UTC IS NOT NULL — courier submitted first application.
Contract Received: TIME_FIRST_CONTRACT_UTC IS NOT NULL — contract was sent to courier.
Contract Signed: TIME_FIRST_SIGNED_UTC IS NOT NULL — courier signed contract.
RTD (Ready To Deliver): TIME_FIRST_READY_UTC IS NOT NULL — courier passed all checks and is ready.
FTD (First Time Delivery): TIME_FIRST_DELIVERY_UTC IS NOT NULL — courier completed first delivery.

⚠️ Stage counts are independent (not strictly nested) — a courier may have contract without signing in some markets. Signed rates are ~17% globally due to market-specific contract flows.
