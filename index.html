Health Score — Full Lifecycle Formula (v6)

Health = 10% Contract Funnel + 20% RTD Rate + 25% Activation + 20% M1 Retention + 15% M2 Retention + 10% Churn Control

• Contract Funnel: % applicants who received a contract. 20–80% → 0–100. (10% weight — noisy across markets)
• RTD Rate: % applicants who reached Ready-To-Deliver. 0–45% → 0–100. (20%)
• Activation: % applicants who completed their first delivery. 0–55% → 0–100. (25%)
• M1 Retention: % couriers with FTD 31–60d ago who are still active today. 40–90% → 0–100. (20%)
• M2 Retention: % couriers with FTD 61–90d ago who are still active today. 20–70% → 0–100. (15%)
• Churn Control: 1 − monthly churn of established active couriers. 5–40% churn → 100–0. (10%)

Retention Cohort Definitions

M0 stock: Couriers whose FTD was in the last 30 days (currently in month 1).
M1 cohort: FTD 31–60 days ago. M1 retention = % who delivered in last 30d.
M2 cohort: FTD 61–90 days ago. M2 retention = % who delivered in last 30d.
Ongoing churn: Couriers active in days -60 to -31 but not in -30 to -1 (established fleet).
